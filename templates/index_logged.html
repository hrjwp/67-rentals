About 67 Rentals

67 Rentals History

Since 2015, 67 Rentals began as a small, family‑run business with just a handful of vehicles and a big vision: to make travel more accessible, flexible, and enjoyable for everyone. The name “67” was chosen to symbolize both the company’s roots along Route 67 — a road known for connecting communities — and its mission to keep people moving forward.

67 Rentals has become a trusted name in the region, known for its modern fleet, customer‑first approach, and commitment to convenience
